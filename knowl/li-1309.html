Item.
If the power of tangent is odd:
\begin{align*} \ds \int \tan^{2k+1}x \sec^n x\, dx \amp = \int \tan^{2k} x\cdot \tan x \cdot \sec^{n-1} x\cdot \sec x\, dx\\ \amp = \ds \int (\tan^2x)^k\cdot\sec^{n-1}x\cdot \sec x\tan x\, dx\\ \amp = \ds \int (\sec^2x-1)^k\cdot \sec^{n-1}x\cdot \sec x\tan x\, dx \end{align*}
Now let \(u = \sec x\text{,}\) simplify, and integrate.
