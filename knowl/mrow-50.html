\begin{align*} \ds \lim_{h\to 0} \dfrac{\sin(\theta + h) - \sin\theta}{h} & = \lim_{h\to 0} \dfrac{\sin\theta \cos h + \sin h \cos\theta - \sin\theta}{h}\\ &= \ds \dfrac{\sin\theta (\cos h -1) + \sin h \cos\theta}{h} \\ &= \ds \sin\theta\cdot \lim_{h\to 0} \dfrac{\cos h -1}{h} + \cos\theta \cdot \lim_{h \to 0} \dfrac{\sin h}{h}\\ &= \cos\theta \end{align*}
