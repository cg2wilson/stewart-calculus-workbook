Item.
If the power of secant is even:
\begin{align*} \ds \int \tan^mx\sec^{2k}x\, dx \amp = \int \tan^m x\sec^{2k-2}\cdot \sec^2x\, dx\\ \amp = \ds \int \tan^m x (\sec^2x)^{k-1}\cdot \sec^2 x\, dx\\ \amp = \ds \int \tan^m x (1+\tan^2x)^{k-1}\cdot \sec^2 x\, dx \end{align*}
Now set \(u = \tan x\text{,}\) simplify, and integrate.
